Kirsten's Kitchen

Cooking can feel like a chore, but it doesn't have to. I've found the more you cook the easier and more fun it gets! Follow along as I try new recipes that turn out great and not so great.

My Menu This Week

Since its getting colder I've got some warm and hearty dishes on the list this week!

  • Monday

    Healthy Turkey Chili

    This one is a proven crowd pleaser for my house and its super easy which makes it a hit with me! Add some sweet cornbread for a true comfort food night.

  • Tuesday

    Veggie Lover's Baked Rigatoni

    With so much cheese you can hardly tell it's just veggies in there! This recipe makes a ton so theres no shame in freezing half for easy meals down the road.

  • Wednesday

    Chicken Fajita Soup

    Totilla soup's healthier cousin, or it just doesn't tell you to add the chips. This is one great but be weary of adding too much salt. We accidentally seasoned before tasting and it was sad, so learn from my mistakes!

  • Thursday

    Vegtable Lo Mein

    You definitely want to double the sauce with this one, and if possible marinate the tofu before time so it doesn't just taste like tofu.

  • Friday

    Leftover Night!

    After a whole week of cooking we all deserve a break (and you don't want to waste all that food!). Pick your favorite from the week and enjoy it all over again!
